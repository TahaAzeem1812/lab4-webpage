Our Vision & Mission

The mission of the trust is:
The core mission of IIMC-T is to address the critical challenge of declining academic standards
within the Muslim Ummah. We aim to achieve this by founding new institutions in Pakistan and enlisting
well-known scholars and scientists in our endeavors.
Our mission places a significant emphasis on nurturing Islamic ethical values among our students, with
the ultimate goal of shaping them into Muslim professionals in fields like
medicine, engineering, and social sciences.

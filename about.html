Introduction

At the Sahiwal Campus, we offer diverse programs designed to meet modern educational and industry
needs. With qualified faculty, well-equipped classrooms, and a focus on practical learning, we ensure that
students gain both theoretical understanding and real-world experience.

Vision and Mission

The mission of the trust is:
The core mission of IIMC-T is to address the critical challenge of declining academic standards within
the Muslim Ummah. We aim to achieve this by founding new institutions in Pakistan and enlisting well-known scholars and
scientists in our endeavors.
Our mission places a significant emphasis on nurturing Islamic ethical values among our students, with the
ultimate goal of shaping them into Muslim professionals in fields like
medicine, engineering, and social sciences.
